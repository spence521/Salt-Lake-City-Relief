Salt Lake City Relief

Would you like to join in on a new revolution to rebuilding this world into a better place? Well hurry in to make a donation that will help thousands of people across the state of Utah. We are building our empire and working on expanding our donation in different cities in the midwest starting with Salt Lake City. The first event we held was at Liberty Park September 26, 2020. We were planning on going to Idaho for another event that fell through due to lack of funds and that is why we are starting this new revolution to help give back to the people for whoever is going through tough times. Please help us out and make a donation to help the cause of our people and our city. Also if you are interested you can purchase our designed masks, hats, shirts, hoodies, and attire. If you have any comments on what you would like to see from our events, please feel free to submit a copy of your request on the About tab. We are also selling beat packs to support local artists that want to build their music career. Feel free to purchase those on Purchase tab. If you do not wish to purchase any beats, you can still submit your email address and a comment below on the About tab. That will add you to a weekly email promo. You can also join the weekly promos by adding your email address (optional) when you donate on the Donate tab. Thank you all for your love and support!
